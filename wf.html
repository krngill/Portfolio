Wireframe

Overview:

Spoon & Spice is a restaurant website designed to provide users
with an engaging and informative experience. The wireframes include
four key pages: Home, Dinner, Lunch, and Info. Each page serves a distinct
purpose to enhance user interaction and provide relevant information about the restaurant.

Home Page:

The Home Page is the entry point for users and sets the tone for the
Spoon&Spice experience. Key features include:
Restaurant Name and Logo: Prominently displayed to establish brand identity.
Opening Hours: Clearly stated to inform users about the operational hours of the restaurant.
Search Options: Intuitive search functionality for users to explore the menu efficiently.
Navigation Links: Clearly defined navigation links for easy access to other sections of the website.


2. Dinner Page:
The Dinner Page showcases the comprehensive dinner menu, providing users with
a delightful culinary journey. Features include:
Starters, Main Course, and Drinks Sections: Clearly segmented to allow users to
navigate through the diverse dinner offerings effortlessly.
Alcoholic and Non-Alcoholic Drinks: Differentiated to cater to varied preferences.
High-Quality Images: Appealing visuals accompany each menu item to entice users.


3. Lunch Page:
The Lunch Page focuses on the lunchtime offerings with a concise and
well-organized layout. Highlights include:
Starters, Main Course, and Drinks Sections: Similar to the Dinner Page
but without alcoholic beverages during lunch hours.
Clear Sectional Differentiation: Ensures that users can quickly locate their preferred items.
Visually Appealing Design: Consistent with the overall theme, maintaining a visually appealing
and user-friendly interface.

4. Info Page:
The Info Page serves as a hub for important details about the restaurant.
Key components include:
Contact Information: Phone numbers, email addresses, and physical location details.
Reservation Information: Guidelines for making reservations for a seamless dining experience.
Job Applications: Information on job opportunities at Culinary Haven.
Takeout Orders: A streamlined process for users interested in takeout options.

Purpose and Audience:

Culinary Haven's website aims to cater to both potential customers looking for a dining experience
and individuals interested in job opportunities. The wireframes are crafted with a user-centric
approach to meet the needs of these diverse audiences.
