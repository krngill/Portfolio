Shop With Us

<

Introduction:

ShopWithUs is an e-commerce website designed to provide users with
a seamless online shopping experience. The project includes
various functionalities such as user registration, product
insertion and display, user authentication, and more.
The website is built using HTML, CSS, PHP, and MySQL,
with Bootstrap for front-end styling..

Key Features:

1.User Registration and Login:
Users can register on the platform by providing their
email, password, name, phone number, and address.
Registered users can log in to access personalized features and make purchases.

2.Product Management:
Products are managed through a MySQL database, with details such
as product name, price, and image stored.
Admins can insert new products into the system, including uploading product images.

3.Product Display:
The website displays a range of products with details such as name,
price, and images. Users can view product details and images by clicking on the respective items.

4.Product Editing:
Admins have the ability to edit product information, including updating the
product name, price, and image.
The product editing functionality ensures that the product database stays up-to-date.

Outcome:

ShopWithUs provides a robust e-commerce platform with user-friendly interfaces for
both customers and administrators. The project demonstrates proficiency in web development,
database management, and security implementation..
